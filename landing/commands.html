Commands

Discord commands and the in-app wallet Activity work together.

Tipping

React 💰 Confirm in Activity

React on any message to tip the author. Opens the Activity to confirm and fund the tip with blind tokens.

/tip @user amount Confirm in Activity

Tip someone directly. Creates a pending tip — open the Activity to fund it.

/settip amount

Set your default tip amount for reaction tips (default: 0.01 ZEC). Only you know this value.

/rain amount

Start a rain event. Anyone who reacts with 🌧️ within 2 hours gets a pending tip to claim.

Anonymous

/anontip @user Wallet-to-wallet

Get a ZIP-321 payment link. Send directly from Zodl or any Zcash wallet. The bot never sees the transaction.

/setaddress z1abc...

Share your Zcash z-address so others can send you anonymous tips via /anontip.

Wallet Activity

Open Activity In-Discord panel

Your wallet runs inside Discord as an embedded app. All crypto happens in your browser — the server never sees your tokens.

Deposit

Get a shielded z-address, send ZEC, then mint blind tokens in your browser.

Balance

Stored locally in your browser. The server doesn't know your balance.

Withdraw

Burn blind tokens and receive ZEC to any z-address via the shielded pool.

Claim Tips

See pending inbound tips and claim them. Your browser mints fresh blind tokens.

Backup

Export your tokens as JSON. If you clear browser data, tokens are gone — back up regularly.

Community

/leaderboard

Ranked by number of tips — not amounts. See who's most generous and most appreciated.
